Captain Planet Needs Your Help!

Greenhouse gases are one of the main contributors to rising temperatures in today’s world. Although the earth’s temperature and atmosphere composition naturally cycle, human activity within the last century has caused a large disturbance in that cycle (see NASA’s page on climate change for more information).

Here is a map that shows CO2 emissions from 2007-2013 for 15 countries across the world. Increases and decreases among several countries can be seen during this time. You can also see what grade each country gets based on their climate change policy. These grades were determined by Climate Action Network Europe in an assessment from 2016. This assessment also ranks countries on other aspects as well. You can see where your country falls here.

We as a world can always improve on how we treat the planet. If you are unsatisfied with your country’s rank, find out ways to contact your leaders or representatives and tell them how important it is to keep our planet healthy for generations to come.
